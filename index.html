Bhargav Kumar Soothram

Robotics Graduate Student @ University of Maryland

Hello there!

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Robotics at the University of Maryland. My primary research interests lie at the intersection of Computer Vision and Deep Learning. Currently, I am invested in Generative Models, where I am working on Image Compression using Gaussian Diffusion.

Prior to my graduate studies at UMD, I was worked as a Data Science Engineer at Mahindra & Mahindra, where I along with my team designed Mahindra Truck and Bus Division's first big-data telematics platform using Google Cloud Services, which reduced the time taken for data analysis significantly.

In my spare time, I like to read books, travel, play video games and badminton.

  • Domain Skills: Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Numerical Optimization, Path Planning, Robot Modeling and Control
  • Programming Languages: Python, C++, MATLAB
  • Libraries and Tools: OpenCV, Pytorch, Keras/Tensorflow, ROS, Google Cloud Platform, Git
